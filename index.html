Formula notes / assumptions

v5.7.a logic: Minimum Required Fuel is treated as required operational fuel. The tool only evaluates fuel carried above Minimum Required Fuel as economic tanker fuel.

Actual validator: Actual carry penalty = ((Actual Out − Actual In) − Planned Leg Fuel) ÷ (Actual Out − (Planned Leg Fuel + Non-Tanker Arrival Fuel)) ÷ Actual Flight Time × 100.

This is a planning aid only. Verify W&B, landing weight, runway analysis, reserves, MEL/CDL, fuel release, and company SOPs.

Not for operational release by itself.
